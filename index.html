Quartet Exercises

Welcome to Quartet Study Resources! The exercises provided here are for use with Quartet: Intermediate Japanese Across the Four Language Skills textbooks and are meant to help you practice what you have learned in each lesson. Select a lesson from the quick navigation and then the exercise that you want to practice for that lesson to begin testing your knowledge. Happy studying!

If you're not sure on how to tackle each lesson, please see our study guide.
