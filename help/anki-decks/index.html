Anki Decks for Quartet

Here you can download Anki decks for Quartet: Intermediate Japanese Across the Four Language Skills, based on the vocabulary/kanji learned in the Quartet textbooks. Click any of the links in the sections below to download a deck.


Quick Navigation

  • Quartet Vocab Decks
  • Quartet Kanji Decks
  • How to Import the Decks

If you would like to download all of the decks at once, click here to download the repository. Once downloaded, unzip the contents, then navigate to resources/tools/decks or resources/tools/kanji and open the lessons folder to access the decks.

You can also find Excel vocabulary lists for Quartet on the official website here!

How to Import the Decks

Once you have your decks, open Anki and click "Import File" (or go to File > Import...) to import your decks OR just drag and drop them into the Anki window.

That's it, you're done. Now you're ready to study the vocab or kanji for Quartet!
